About This Build

This ground-up custom residence in Little Rock's Bear Den neighborhood showcases CRA Construction's hallmark blend of bold exterior design and refined interior craftsmanship. The striking dark sage farmhouse exterior — featuring board-and-batten siding, black window frames, and a standing-seam metal roof — creates immediate curb presence while harmonizing with the natural tree line.

Inside, open-concept living areas flow seamlessly from a chef's kitchen through to covered outdoor spaces, with custom cabinetry, wide-plank hardwood floors, and statement lighting throughout. Every detail — from the spa-style primary suite to the curated fixtures — was selected to deliver a home that's both livable and enduring.
